Fendt 724 Vario Tractor equipped with multi-modal sensor setup.

Abstract

We present a large-scale (25TB and growing) multi-modal dataset containing raw environmental data collected during routine farming operations. The data was continuously recorded using a custom-built, rugged sensor setup mounted on a Fendt 724 Vario tractor.

This dataset is designed to support research in agricultural robotics, autonomous navigation in unstructured environments, computer vision, environmental mapping, and multi-modal sensor fusion.

We are continuously expanding it to include a broader range of agricultural tasks, varying environmental conditions that affect signal quality and visibility, different crop types and growth stages, as well as diverse objects encountered in the field.

Data Characteristics

  • Location: Research Farm of the Leipzig University (Lehr- und Forschungsgut Oberholz)
  • Seasons: Currently Spring and Summer; will be expanded to include multiple growing seasons.
  • Tasks: plowing, tilling, cultivating, mowing; will be expanded to include planting, harvesting, fertilizing and more.

LiDAR Point Clouds

The Ouster OS0 LiDAR (128 scanlines) is mounted forward-facing with a 45째 downward tilt at a height of 3 meters. This configuration captures a high-resolution 3D point cloud of the environment, supporting precise mapping and localization, object detection, and use as a deterministic safety sensor.
